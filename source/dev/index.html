Our Vision of «Play-to-earn»

Our marketplace gives players the opportunity to earn money on AAA games that are already firmly established on the market. These games already have hundreds of millions of players enjoying the gameplay, making in-game goods and services tied to those games valuable. Making money on your favorite game with an already huge client base is much more efficient and enjoyable than making money on so-called play-to-earn casual games that are designed to extort money from users and are completely uninteresting.

Upcoming improvements

We aim to keep constantly improving our service and adding new features. Our goal is creating a metaverse for gamers, which will encompass all gaming worlds. We will make Loot4Pay the go-to place among all gamers for anything you might need in ANY game.
Automation skins trade
Implementation of special functionality that will allow people to trade in-game skins in Dota 2 and CS:GO games automatically. It will save seller’s and buyer’s time and make their user experience super enjoyable.
Paid Features
We will introduce the possibility for the sellers to advertise and customize their offers for a fee. This will help new merchants to gain a foothold on the
platform.
Presentation of the lottery
Tickets, required to participate in the lottery, can be purchased only with L4P token. The more tickets one has, the higher the chance of the victory.
Teammate Live Search
We will implement a live search function. Placing orders to find teammate, professional or mentor will be just as easy as calling a taxi using a phone app.
Metaverse for Gaming
Loot4Pay will become a complete social network for gamers. They will be able to use it to locate other people with similar interests and find new friends to play with or discuss any game related topic.
Streaming Platform
Adding a streaming service similar to Twitch.tv. Live streaming is an integral part of gaming culture and we we are going to use it to our advantage.
continuous Interface Improvement
Updating the user interface is high on our priority list. A quality UI/UX is important for both the overall user experience and presentation. We will never stop improving it.

Token Utility

The Loot4Pay token will be used to pay for goods and services provided by the sellers using our marketplace, as well as for other miscellaneous purchases like pins and customization for offers, lottery tickets etc. We are going to introduce an 8-15% discount for all transactions made using the l4p token. We will continue to grow the platform to provide additional spending opportunities, adding value to the token.

Token Burning

All of the additional options offered by marketplace will be available for purchase exclusively via our token, and half tokens we get this way will be burned every month to decrease circulating supply. In addition, 10% of tokens acquired from purchases of additional features as well as lottery tickets will also be burned, to reduce the total amount in circulation.
